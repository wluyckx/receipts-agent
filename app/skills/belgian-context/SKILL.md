# Belgian Context

## Energy Regulations

### Capacity Tariff (Capaciteitstarief / Kwartierpiek)
- Based on highest 15-minute average power peak per month (kwartierpiek)
- Measured by Fluvius digital meter
- Monthly peak determines the capacity component of the network tariff
- Peak typically occurs when cooking + washing + EV charging simultaneously
- **Strategy**: spread high-consumption activities across time slots
- Tariff component visible on yearly Fluvius settlement (jaarafrekening)

### Prosumer Tariff
- Solar panel owners injecting to grid pay a prosumer tariff
- Net metering (terugdraaiende teller) ended for new installations (2024+)
- Digital meter measures injection and consumption separately
- Compensation via injection tariff, but lower than consumption tariff

### Time-of-Use Tariff (Dag/Nacht Tarief)
- Day rate (dagtarief): Mon-Fri 07:00-22:00
- Night rate (nachttarief): Mon-Fri 22:00-07:00, all weekend, public holidays
- Night rate is ~40% cheaper than day rate
- **Strategy**: schedule dishwasher, washing machine, dryer for night/weekend

### Fluvius
- Flemish distribution system operator (DSO)
- Digital meter rollout: >90% complete in Flanders
- Reads every 15 minutes, data available via Mijn Fluvius portal
- Billing: monthly advance payments + yearly settlement
- Terms: afname (consumption), injectie (injection), piek (peak),
  netkosten (network costs), distributiekosten (distribution costs)

## Retail Landscape

### Colruyt Group Brands
- **Colruyt** — Lowest price guarantee (rode prijzen). Largest Belgian retailer.
  No bags provided. Warehouse-style stores. Xtra loyalty card.
- **Collect&Go** — Colruyt online ordering with store pickup
- **Okay** — Colruyt Group discount format. Smaller stores, neighbourhood focus.
- **Bio-Planet** — Colruyt Group organic/bio supermarket
- **Cru** — Colruyt Group premium/specialty food market
- **Xtra card** — Colruyt Group loyalty program. Savings in cents (xtra_savings_cents
  on receipts). Automatic discount at checkout.

### Other Major Retailers
- **Spar** — Cooperative, neighbourhood supermarkets. Higher prices but convenient.
- **Delhaize** — Premium supermarket chain. Lion brand. Higher quality perception.
- **Lidl** — German discount chain. Weekly promotions (folder).
- **Aldi** — German discount chain. Limited assortment, low prices.
- **Carrefour** — French chain. Hypermarkets and Express formats.

### Belgian Grocery Conventions
- All prices in EUR. Receipt amounts in cents.
- Metric system: kg, g, L, mL
- Product labels typically in Dutch (Flanders) or French (Wallonia)
- Deposit system (statiegeld): €0.25 on PET bottles ≥0.5L (since 2024)
- Sunday shopping: most supermarkets open until 13:00

### Meal Culture
- **Ontbijt** (breakfast): bread, cereal, typically light
- **Middageten** (lunch): often packed sandwiches (boterhammen), warm lunch less common
- **Avondeten** (dinner): main warm meal, typically 18:00-19:00
- **Weekend**: warm lunch more common, BBQ culture in summer
- **Seasonal**: stamppot/stoofvlees in winter, salads/BBQ in summer
- **Belgian specialties**: frietjes (fries), stoofvlees (beef stew), wafels,
  waterzooi, vol-au-vent
